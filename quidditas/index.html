About My Blog: Quidditas

Quidditas is the name I’ve given to my personal blog. My main goal is to post about interesting topics, create educational material, and talk about my current research. Additionally, you can also find tutorials and references that I’ve written about making this website.

The word “quidditas” itself is Latin, with a rather odd definition: quidditas is the answer to the question, “What is the thing?”
